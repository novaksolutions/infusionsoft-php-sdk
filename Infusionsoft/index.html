Installation

Copy the config.sample.php file to config.php and set your APIKey and App Hostname, this needs to be your complete app hostname, i.e. joey.infusionsoft.com

Test your settings by clicking on this link: Test Connectivity

WARNINGS

  • Saving a record when you didn't retrieve all the fields will erase data.
